Homesick in Paradise? Stay in New Salt Lake!

On the southern coast of Taniti lies what we call "little America", if you're homesick you should consider staying in New Salt Lake, Taniti.

This part of the island is a fantastic blend of American and Taniti'an cultures, providing a familiar experience for those who aren't ready to fully embrace the Taniti'an lifestyle, lodging, or diet.
