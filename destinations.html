Mighty Mt. Taniti'tiki

Visit Taniti's famous Taniti'tiki mountain, home of the great spirit Tititiki!

Our guided treks are perfect for hikers of any skill level, walk like a Tanitian all the way to the summit of the Mighty Mt. Taniti'tiki, and commune with our protective island spirit of Tititiki! Upon return, become an honorary Taniti'an for this feat.

Eastern Taniti White Sands

The sun rises in the east, enjoy it from the beautiful white sands of Taniti's eastern port, Kahlua!

Tourists and locals alike cannot resist the allure of Kahlua! Beautiful white sands and clear waters make for the perfect island getaway experience.

Offshore Colonies for those who desire the "real" Tanitian experience

Haven't seen enough of the "real" Taniti? If you're brave enough, venture out into our tropical waters and visit our famous offshore getaways.

Taniti has always been by the sea, and thus has always had a symbotic relationship with the waters which gave us life! Are you up to the challenge?
